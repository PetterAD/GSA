An independent law firm

Gamla Strandgatan Advokatbyrå is an independent Swedish boutique law firm. The firmprovides advice and assistance in all matters relating to commercial disputes, including risk analysis and early case assessments, investigations, litigation in courts and arbitration proceedings, settlement negotiations and mediation. In cases where it is favourable or necessary to compose a team of lawyers, the firm co-operates with several established and well-known law firms in Sweden and abroad. Hence, the firm does not have to choose lawyers from one single firm/brand but can instead put together a team with the lawyers that are most experienced and suitable for the specific case at hand.
